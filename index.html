Story

Welcome to the Floofie Kingdom, The happiest Kingdom on Earth, ruled by Queen Floofie the Cat, and her best friend Mousey!
 
One day, the evil Lord Vacuum of Vacuumville broke into the Queen's castle and kidnapped Mousey.
 
You must play as Queen Floofie to rescue Mousey from Vacuumville on your Hoverboard of Justice!
 
Good luck, and restore peace back to the kingdom!
